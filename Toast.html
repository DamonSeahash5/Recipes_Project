Toast Recipe


About the Recipe

Toast is a delicious and simple recipe, great for hangovers or a quick breakfast. Try topping with your favourite items such as Jam or Marmite


Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter or Spread of choice

Method

  1. Open bread packet
  2. Put bread in toaster
  3. Adjust toaster settings as desired and turn on
  4. Once toaster has pooped up, remove bread to plate
  5. Spread butter on toast
