Meatballs Recipe

About the Recipe

Meatballs are a classic Italian inspired dish, just like Nona used to make

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Herbs
  • Egg
  • Seasoning

Method

  1. Combine all the ingredients thoroughly in a bowl
  2. Spread a tray with flour
  3. Roll the mixture into small (<5cm) balls with your hands and coat evenly with the flour on the tray
  4. Brown the meatballs in a hot pan with some oil, turning frequently
  5. Add to a tomato based sauce of your choice to continue cooking
